Sierra Majesty
National Sanctuary

Established in 1864, Sierra Majesty is renowned as a guardian of nature's grandeur. While it boasts the famed spectacle of cascading waterfalls, the sanctuary's allure extends far beyond, unfolding over nearly 1,200 square miles.

Wander through deep valleys that whisper tales of time, traverse expansive meadows that dance with the winds of history, and stand in awe beneath the towering presence of ancient giant sequoias.?

Sierra Majesty National Sanctuary, a haven that transcends mere landscapes, is a tribute to human foresight and the enduring forces that shape our world. Within the embrace of this sanctuary, witness the sheer strength of granite, the transformative power of glaciers, and the tenacious persistence of life itself amidst the tranquil beauty of the High Sierra.

Sierra Majesty National Sanctuary is not merely a destination; it's a pilgrimage into the heart of natural wonders, where each vista tells a story of resilience, grandeur, and the sublime beauty of the interconnected elements that define this majestic sanctuary.
